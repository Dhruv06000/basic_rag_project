Machine learning has applications across many industries.

In healthcare, it assists with medical image analysis and disease prediction. In finance, it detects fraud and predicts market trends. In natural language processing, it powers chatbots, translation systems, and sentiment analysis tools. In computer vision, it enables object recognition and facial detection.

Machine learning is also used in recommendation systems, autonomous vehicles, predictive maintenance, and cybersecurity.

Its ability to analyze large datasets and identify patterns makes it essential in modern artificial intelligence systems.
